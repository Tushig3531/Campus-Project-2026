Plan

Goal: lower bills + lower emissions.

Step 1 — Comfort
Quick fixes
  • Seal drafts (AC covers)
  • Check insulation / leaks
Step 2 — Metering (Save Money)
Reduce service charges
  • Confirm code + get quotes (multi-tenant rules)
  • If allowed: combine many meters into one meter per building
  • Use the money saved to help pay for solar and heating upgrades
Step 3 — Clean Energy
Solar + heating plan
  • Add rooftop solar (proposal idea: ~27 kW)
  • Pick a heating path (heat pump / geothermal / biogas)
